Who Are You?

Choose a character name and an expertise. For instance: 'geoffrey the pidgeon trainer'

Make up two murder weapons that are closely related to your expertise. For instance: 'pidgeon feather tipped with poison'

Make up four pieces of evidence that relate to your expertise. Can be a motive or a found piece of evidence. For instance: 'found: pidgeon coreography sheet' or 'motive: Guy Fieri cooked a pidgeon on his show last night'
